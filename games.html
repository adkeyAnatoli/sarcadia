    Online Casino Slots FAQ

    ​

    How to choose the best slot game?​

    ​

    Honestly there's no specific answer to this question. There are a lot of factors that can affect your decision when it comes to which slot is best; first being your bankroll and second - the type of games you enjoy. Due to the large amount and variety of available slot games, figuring out which one is worth playing can be quite time consuming. The differentiating factors between slots can be type of grid (5x3, 7x7 etc), jackpot presence, type of animation, volatility, popularity etc. So the decision is yours.

    ​

    Can I play slots for free?​

    ​

    Most online casinos allow you to try their games for free, offering a demo version of the slot. This is a great opportunity to try out a new game, explore its features and potential, and see if this slot works for you before actually spending your money since playing a demo version is completely free and won't reduce your balance. 

    ​

    What's the difference between Video Slots and Classic Slot?​

    ​

    The difference between both are the reels, payline and also the bonus feature. Nowadays, most of the slots you see in online casinos are video slots, which include graphic and design (good ones in most cases) and is quite different from the plain layout or reels slots. Normally regular slots are easily identifiable since they're generally 3 reel-games with a single payout. Regular slots feature symbols like fruit, sevens, bells and the paytable would be shown on the main screen. Another big difference is the bonus feature - video slots have several different bonus features and free spin games while regular slots have no more than one simple bonus game. Lastly, video slots offer games with a variety of themes, minimum 5 reels and up to 1000 paylines, in addition, they can be branded and based on movies, tv show, pop culture etc.

    ​

    What's a Progressive Jackpot Slot?​

    ​

    It generally refers to those slot games which include a jackpot so it gives you the possibility of an huge payout. The more players wager on that slot the bigger the jackpot gets. In other words, the more a player loses the more jackpot grows. Take in mind that, since the potential winning can reach even millions, the regular payouts are lower. The jackpot is reset as soon as a player wins.

    ​

    What is RTP?​

    ​

    Also known as Return To Player, the RTP indicates the % of your investment which will return to you in a form of winning. In simple words, if a slot game has an RTP of 96%, after every 100 euro investment, you would potentially get back 96 euro, leaving 4 euro as casino profit. The higher the RTP, the bigger is the potential pay out, however it would be less frequent. On average the RTP of online slots is around 94% and 97%.

    ​

    How much can a slot game pay?​

    ​

    The maximum that a slog game can pay is generally mentioned in the intro of the game or in the game info area. The bigger is the maximum multiplier the larger can be your winnings. A slot game with a maximum payout equal to 5000x means that you can win up to 5000x your bet, though there are slot games with much bigger potential - over 100.000x.
