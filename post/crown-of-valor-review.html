    Crown of Valor | Welcome to the Dark Ages |☆ 4.4/5

    Updated: Mar 14

    One of the latest releases by QuickSpin which will bring you back to the Dark Ages with this animated 5 reels slot game. This cluster pay game benefits from amazing 3D graphics and an epic soundtrack, which will make you travel back in time and get a taste of medieval era (or you can watch Game of Thrones, it's very realistic and historically accurate). For each cluster pay, the multiplier goes up by 1 and also every win adds a burning wild into the game. Your goal is to fill the meter with 4 burning wilds, which will grant you access to the Free Spin bonus game, where you have a chance to play potentially unlimited spin, in addition increasing the multiplier with each win. One word - epic.

    Crown of Valor Bonus Features

    Tumble Feature

    Tumble Feature can happen in both regular game and bonus round, the tumble mechanism removes all the winning symbols from the grid and leaves space to new ones, that way increasing your chances to get another winning. The tumble continues until a new winning is generated. When no winning clusters appear on the screen, tumble feature ends.

    Burning Wild feature

    Every time you get a winning spin, 1 burning wild appears on the grid and is randomly placed in one of the columns. The burning wild will substitute any symbol except the bonus scatter, and every time you get a winning with the burning wild, the multiplier meter will increase by 1. Afterwards if you get another winning, an additional burning wild will be added to the grid following the same process as described previously.

    Wild Fire feature

    If you successfully fill the meter gauge completely in the base game, you activate the Wild Fire feature. Basically, 4 Burning Wilds will be placed randomly on the reels, except on existing Bonus scatter and Wild symbols.

    In general Crown of Valor is an extremely volatile 5 x 5 slot game with clusters of 5 or more symbols. The maximum payout is 15.500x of your bet. Not bad! 18 winning tumble will fill the meter and award you a Bonus round. However, if you want to skip the regular game (ain't nobody got time fo' that), you have an option to buy the bonus which costs 80x your bet.

    Free Spins Bonus

    3 Scatter symbols in a single spin will award 10 Free Spins.

    4 Scatter symbols in a single spin will award 14 Free Spins.

    5 Scatter symbols in a single spin will award 18 Free Spins.

    If you get 1 additional bonus scatter during the bonus round, you will add 1 more free spin, with no limits. Don't forget that any winning spin increases the multiplier by 1. However, the multiplier can only increase and it won't reset to 1x. In addition, any Burning Wild symbol that is part of a win also increases the multiplier by 1 (I promise it's not as complicated as I might make it sound).

    Lastly, you can opt for the Buy feature which costs 80x your stake ('cause, like I said, ain't nobody got time for a regular game). This gives you immediate access to the Free Spins Bonus feature. The number of Scatter you can award is random and can be 3, 4 or 5 bonus scatters.

    RTP, Stakes and more

    As already mentioned earlier, Crown of Valor is a 5 x 5 slot and each win is generated by creating a cluster of 5 or more symbols. The bigger is the cluster, the better is your winning. The available stakes are €0.20 to €100 per single spin and it can easily be changed by pressing buttons -/+ at the bottom of the screen. You can also enjoy the fast mode by select Quick Spin mode ('CAUSE AIN'T. NOBODY. GOT. TIME. FO'. THAT) and Autoplay up to 1000 spin. A very useful option that can help you gamble responsibly is win and loss limit which you can find in the advanced settings.

    The RTP of the slot is 96.16% during the regular game round (which is slightly higher than the average 96%) and it becomes 96.48% during the Free Spin round.

    The maximum payout is 15.500x your bet and the key for big winnings is collecting big clusters and high multipliers.
